CSE 131 FINAL

My theme for this project is "Drive". I took photos during a roadtrip to Steptoe Butte, however a majority of the photos are taken on the road there. The intention behind this was to illustrate that the best part of a roadtrip is during the drive, rather than the destination.

All photos were taken at Steptoe Butte, Columbia River, and on the road between the two.
